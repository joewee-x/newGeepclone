GEEP

The Government Enterprise and Empowerment Programme (GEEP) is a flagship initiative under Nigeria's National Social Investment Programme Agency (NSIPA). Created to enable financial inclusion and economic empowerment, GEEP provides zero-interest, collateral-free loans to smallholder farmers, petty traders, artisans, and market women across the country.
